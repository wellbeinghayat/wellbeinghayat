Mission & vision

Why Wellbeing Hayat exists

Our mission

To design and deliver mobile apps that genuinely improve the quality of everyday human life — making healthcare, faith, family connection, and personal growth more accessible, meaningful, and simple for people everywhere. We build technology that serves people, not the other way around.

Our vision

A world where thoughtful technology bridges the gap between modern life and human flourishing. We envision a suite of apps — trusted by families, guided by values, and integrated with the devices people already use — that help every person live healthier, more connected, and more intentional lives.
